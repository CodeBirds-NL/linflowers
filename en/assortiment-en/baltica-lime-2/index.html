Baltica Lime

Baltica Lime

The Baltica Lime has a unique green-white colour. This gives a fresh look to the bouquet.

Lime combines well with soft colours such as salmon. With their green colour, they are therefore often used in the spring.

The Baltica is part of a strong family: strong in transport and strong in the vase!

Availability: Year round
Size of the flower: 8-9 centimetres
Branch length: 70 centimetres
Branch weight: 90-95 grams

Exclusively available at Linflowers!

Remove these flowers from the box as soon as possible. Cut the stem a bit and put it in water. Give the flowers some space so that they last as long as possible. The Baltica’s are best stored at 5-10 degrees.
